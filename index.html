About Vue

Vue is an educational tool designed to raise awareness about colorblindness and help people understand how those with different types of color vision deficiency perceive the world.

Our mission is to promote inclusive design and accessibility by providing tools that simulate various types of colorblindness.

Types of Colorblindness

  • Protanopia: Red-blind, reduced sensitivity to red light
  • Deuteranopia: Green-blind, reduced sensitivity to green light
  • Tritanopia: Blue-blind, reduced sensitivity to blue light
